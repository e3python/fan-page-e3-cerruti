About Our Community

Being an e3 scholar is about more than just grades. It is about character. We are task-oriented, we take calculated risks, and we consistently embrace challenges to push ourselves further. We define ourselves by the GRIFFIN acronym.

The 7 Traits of a Griffin

Every scholar strives to embody these core values every day:

  • Growth: We push ourselves further and embrace challenges.
  • Respect: We maintain professionalism and always act with kindness.
  • Integrity: We are authentic, honest, and avoid plagiarism.
  • Flexibility: We work with different people and are open to new ideas.
  • Focus: We keep our phones away and practice active listening.
  • Innovative: We are resourceful, creative, and take charge of our learning.
  • Non-Violence: We are upstanders, not bystanders.

Classroom Expectations

To maintain a safe learning environment, Griffins always keep their hands to themselves and respect the personal space of others. When we make mistakes, we are apologetic and take responsibility.

We stay in our designated areas and ensure our ears are clear to hear instructions. By doing this, we show that we are ready to learn and grow.
